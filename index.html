About Thea

The vision behind the brush, dedicated to artistry and precision.

"Beauty is not about transformation, but illumination."

With a decade of experience spanning Paris Fashion Week, major film sets, and exclusive celebrity red carpets, Thea brings a cinematic perspective to every face. Her signature style, "Glam Noir," is defined by clean lines, rich color saturation, and the perfect blend of high-fashion structure with soft, feminine glow.

Thea believes in meticulous preparation and a tailored approach, ensuring every client feels authentically themselves, only elevated. Her work has been featured in Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, and numerous global advertising campaigns. Collaboration is at the heart of her process, working closely with designers and photographers to realize the full artistic vision.
